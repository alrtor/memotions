Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click on the "Sign Up" button, enter your email, create a username and password, and you're ready to start exploring Memotions!
How do I upload a meme or GIF?
Click the "+" icon in the top navigation, select your image or GIF file, add a caption and tags, then click "Post". Your content will be live immediately!
Can I edit or delete my post?
Yes! Go to your profile, find the post you want to edit or delete, click the three dots menu, and select the appropriate option.
How do reactions work?
You can react to any post with various emojis like 😂, 🔥, 💀, and more. Your most-used reactions will appear on your profile. Reactions help content go viral!
Is Memotions free?
Yes! Memotions is completely free to use. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core experience will always be free.
How do I report inappropriate content?
Click the three dots menu on any post and select "Report". Our moderation team will review it within 24 hours.

Community Guidelines

  • Be Respectful: Treat others with kindness and respect. Harassment, hate speech, and bullying are strictly prohibited.
  • Original Content: Share your own memes or properly credit creators. Don't post copyrighted material without permission.
  • No NSFW Content: Keep it clean and appropriate for all ages. Explicit or violent content will be removed.
  • No Spam: Don't post repetitive content, excessive promotions, or irrelevant links.
  • Report Responsibly: Use the report feature for genuine violations, not for content you simply disagree with.
  • Stay On Topic: Memotions is for memes and GIFs. Keep your content relevant to the platform's purpose.

Violations of these guidelines may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban.
